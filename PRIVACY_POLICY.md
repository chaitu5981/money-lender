# Privacy Policy for Money Lenders Calculator

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

Money Lenders Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

Our app stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- Interest rate settings
- Transaction data (borrowals and repayments)
- Calculation dates
- Theme preferences (light/dark mode)

**This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.**

### No Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

- Personal information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Any data that leaves your device

## Data Storage

All data entered in the app is stored locally on your device using:

- AsyncStorage (React Native's local storage)
- Your device's internal storage

## Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transmit any data to:

- Third-party services
- Analytics providers
- Advertising networks
- Any external servers

## Data Security

- All data remains on your device
- No network transmission of your data
- No cloud storage or backup (unless you manually export)

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally:

- You have full control over your data
- You can delete the app to remove all data
- You can clear app data from device settings

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children. The app is designed for general use.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
